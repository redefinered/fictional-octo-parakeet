Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

ParkU ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (optional).
  • Parking Spot Information: When you add a parking spot, we collect location data (coordinates), address, safety features, and photos (optional).
  • Reviews and Ratings: We collect your reviews, ratings, and comments about parking spots.
  • Payment Information: For future payment features, we may collect payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors).

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: We collect your device's location (with your permission) to show nearby parking spots and provide navigation services.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including parking history, saved spots, and app interactions.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and app version for technical support and improvements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our parking finding services
  • Display nearby parking spots based on your location
  • Enable community features like reviews and ratings
  • Process payments and reservations (when available)
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Public Information: Parking spots you add, reviews, and ratings are visible to other users of the app.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase, Google Maps) who assist in operating our app.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

5. Location Data

Location data is essential for our app to function. We only collect location data when you grant permission. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

Location data is used to:

  • Show nearby parking spots on the map
  • Provide navigation directions to parking spots
  • Allow you to add new parking spots at your current location

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and storage services
  • Google Maps: For map display and navigation features
  • Payment Processors: For processing payments (GCash, PayPal, Stripe) when payment features are available

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information through the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or notifications through device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@parku.app
Website: parku.app
