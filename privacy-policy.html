Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

ParkU ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Compliance with Philippine Law: This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations. We adhere to the principles of transparency, legitimate purpose, and proportionality in processing your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (optional).
  • Parking Spot Information: When you add a parking spot, we collect location data (coordinates), address, safety features, and photos (optional).
  • Reviews and Ratings: We collect your reviews, ratings, and comments about parking spots.
  • Payment Information: For future payment features, we may collect payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors).

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: We collect your device's location (with your permission) to show nearby parking spots and provide navigation services.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including parking history, saved spots, and app interactions.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and app version for technical support and improvements.

2. Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act, we process your personal data based on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent for specific data processing activities
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our obligations under the terms of service
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and ensure security
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We use the information we collect for the following legitimate purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our parking finding services
  • Display nearby parking spots based on your location
  • Enable community features like reviews and ratings
  • Process payments and reservations (when available)
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights

Principle of Proportionality: We only collect and process personal data that is adequate, relevant, and necessary for the declared purposes. We do not collect excessive or unnecessary data.

3. Data Storage and Security Measures

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). In compliance with the Data Privacy Act, we implement appropriate technical, physical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

3.1 Technical Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems

3.2 Organizational Security Measures

  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Employee training on data privacy and security
  • Confidentiality agreements with personnel
  • Regular review and update of security policies

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

3.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that may pose a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the National Privacy Commission (NPC) and affected data subjects within 72 hours of discovery, in accordance with the Data Privacy Act. We will also take immediate steps to mitigate the breach and prevent further unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Public Information: Parking spots you add, reviews, and ratings are visible to other users of the app. This sharing is necessary for the app's core functionality.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase, Google Maps) who assist in operating our app. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

4.1 Cross-Border Data Transfer

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside the Philippines (e.g., Google Cloud Platform servers). When we transfer data across borders, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Contractual agreements with data processors that include data protection clauses
  • Verification that the receiving country has adequate data protection laws, or
  • Implementation of additional security measures to protect your data

By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations, subject to the safeguards described above.

5. Location Data

Location data is essential for our app to function. We only collect location data when you grant permission. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

Location data is used to:

  • Show nearby parking spots on the map
  • Provide navigation directions to parking spots
  • Allow you to add new parking spots at your current location

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and storage services
  • Google Maps: For map display and navigation features
  • Payment Processors: For processing payments (GCash, PayPal, Stripe) when payment features are available

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

7. Your Rights Under the Data Privacy Act

As a data subject under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the following rights:

7.1 Right to be Informed

You have the right to be informed about the collection and processing of your personal data. This Privacy Policy serves to fulfill this right.

7.2 Right to Access

You have the right to access and obtain a copy of your personal data that we hold. You may request access to your data at any time.

7.3 Right to Object

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, including processing for direct marketing, automated processing, or profiling. You may withdraw your consent at any time, subject to legal and contractual restrictions.

7.4 Right to Erasure or Blocking

You have the right to request the deletion or blocking of your personal data if it is incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained, used for unauthorized purposes, or no longer necessary for the declared purpose.

7.5 Right to Damages

You have the right to claim damages if you suffer due to inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained, or unauthorized use of your personal data.

7.6 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to obtain and electronically move, copy, or transfer your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

7.7 Right to File a Complaint

You have the right to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your data privacy rights have been violated. You may contact the NPC at:

  • Website: privacy.gov.ph
  • Email: info@privacy.gov.ph
  • Hotline: (02) 8234-2228

7.8 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable time, not exceeding 30 days, as required by the Data Privacy Act.

Note: We may require proof of identity before processing your request to ensure the security of your personal data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Parental Consent: If we discover that we have collected personal data from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will delete that information promptly in accordance with the Data Privacy Act.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Our data retention policy is as follows:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days after account deletion, unless required for legal purposes.
  • Location Data: Retained only during active app sessions and not stored permanently unless you add a parking spot.
  • Reviews and Ratings: Retained as long as the associated parking spot exists, unless you request deletion.
  • Payment Data: Retained as required by law for financial record-keeping (typically 7 years).
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain certain data longer if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

When data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with the Data Privacy Act.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Consent

By using our app and services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us or deleting your account, subject to legal and contractual restrictions.

Withdrawal of Consent: If you withdraw consent, we will stop processing your personal data, except where we have a legal basis to continue processing (e.g., legal obligation, contractual necessity).
